Sound Bloom Studio

Privacy Policy

Effective date: To be set before release

Sound Bloom Studio is provided by KP Studio.

This Privacy Policy explains what information Sound Bloom Studio handles and how it is used.

Information We Handle

Sound Bloom Studio may handle the following information on your device:

  • Microphone audio, when you use the tuner, wave view, or recording features
  • Audio recordings that you create in the app
  • Audio files that you choose to import
  • App settings, such as reference pitch, sensitivity, input threshold, and note name style
  • Recording metadata, such as file name, creation date, duration, and local file path

How We Use Information

Sound Bloom Studio uses this information only to provide app features, including:

  • Detecting pitch
  • Displaying tuning and pitch history
  • Recording and playing back audio
  • Importing audio files selected by you
  • Saving your app settings
  • Managing your local recording list

Local Storage

Audio recordings, imported audio files, app settings, and recording metadata are stored locally on your device.

Sound Bloom Studio does not upload your recordings, imported files, microphone audio, app settings, or recording metadata to a server operated by KP Studio.

Data Sharing

Sound Bloom Studio does not sell your personal information.

Sound Bloom Studio does not include third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or crash reporting services.

Microphone Permission

Sound Bloom Studio requests microphone access to detect pitch and record audio. Microphone access is used only while related app features are in use.

File Access

Sound Bloom Studio may ask you to select an audio file when you use the import feature. Only the file you choose is imported into the app.

Data Deletion

You can delete recordings from within the app. Deleting a recording removes it from the app's recording list and deletes the associated local audio file when available.

You may also remove app data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data through your device settings.

Children's Privacy

Sound Bloom Studio is a general music practice tool and is not directed specifically to children. KP Studio does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the effective date.
